# MytheAi Backlink Outreach Playbook (May 2026)

Operational document. Not user-facing. Use this to run a 90-day outreach sprint to lift Authority signals beyond what code can deliver.

## Why this exists

Code-side wins (S77+ rounds) pushed UI/UX, SEO, Content, and Monetization to ~9.0. Authority remains the bottleneck because it requires off-site signals: domain mentions, third-party links, brand recognition. Search engines do not award authority for clean schema alone; they award it for who else links to you.

**Target outcome (90 days):** 25+ contextual backlinks from AI/SaaS-relevant publications, 3+ data-study citations, 1+ podcast guest spot.

These exist in the live site and are the angles you pitch *with*:

1. **State of AI Tools 2026 data study** - `/blog/state-of-ai-tools-2026` - original research with 8 data findings. The strongest link bait we have.

## Do-not pattern

- **Never use** generic "guest post" pitches. Most editors block these by spam filter or autoresponder.
- **Never offer** to "exchange links" - 2026 SEO penalises this and editors find it cheap.
- **Never paraphrase** the data study findings as if they came from elsewhere; always cite the source URL.
- **Never pay** for links. Worse than no links - Google detects this and penalties cascade.
- **Never blast** the same email unchanged to multiple recipients. Personalise to context, even slightly.

## What works in 2026

- **Original data with charts** - the State of AI Tools report is built specifically to be linkable. Lean on findings, not aggregate stats.
- **Concrete tool comparisons** when journalists need a third-party reference - "MytheAi rated A 4.6/5 vs B 4.4/5" is more interesting than "A is better than B".
- **Glossary references** - cheap link wins because publishers prefer external references to inline definitions.
- **Personal email** from John Ethan address (not "team@") with one-line context that proves you read the recipient's work.

## Measurement

- Track new referring domains in Google Search Console weekly.
- Track domain rating change in Ahrefs (or free Moz alternative) monthly.
- Track organic traffic delta on `/blog/state-of-ai-tools-2026` - data study pages get traffic from referring domains' link juice quickly.

## Done is better than perfect

Aim for 100 sends in 90 days, not 50 perfect ones. The ROI on the 60th decent email is non-zero; the ROI on polishing the 5 best ones to 110% is zero. Volume + relevance beats perfection.
