The phrase "free AI tools" is mostly a trap in 2026. The advertised free tiers on most marketing pages are demos that quit working the moment you do real work. But the genuine free options - tools where the free tier is generous enough to run a solopreneur business on - exist, and stacking them produces a $0/month workflow that handles 70-80% of what a $200/mo paid stack does.

This guide covers the AI tools with truly usable free tiers in 2026, organised by workflow, with the honest trade-offs.

## Writing and reasoning

### [ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt) Free

The free ChatGPT tier in 2026 includes GPT-5 with daily message limits (~10-15/day on heavy use), DALL-E image generation (3-5 per day), and basic browsing. Sufficient for solopreneurs writing email copy, drafting blog outlines, and running occasional research queries.

**Trade-off vs Plus ($20/mo):** rate limits, no Custom GPTs, slower model on heavy use.

### [Claude](/tools/claude) Free

[Claude](/tools/claude) Free tier includes Claude Sonnet 4.5 with daily message limits. Genuinely capable - the same model the Pro tier uses, just with a daily cap. For solopreneurs doing 1-2 hours of writing per day, the free tier is sufficient.

**Trade-off vs Pro ($20/mo):** ~30 messages/day limit, no Projects feature, no Computer Use.

### [Gemini](/tools/gemini) Free

[Gemini](/tools/gemini) Free in 2026 is the most generous of the three. Includes Gemini 2.5 Pro with a higher rate limit than ChatGPT or Claude free tiers, plus image generation via Imagen.

**Trade-off vs Advanced ($20/mo):** smaller context window, no Workspace integration, no Deep Research mode.

**Recommendation for solopreneurs:** [Gemini](/tools/gemini) Free as default + [Claude](/tools/claude) Free for analytical writing.

## Coding

### [Windsurf](/tools/windsurf) Free

[Windsurf](/tools/windsurf) (formerly Codeium) Free tier is the most generous of any AI coding tool: unlimited inline completions, no daily caps, basic Cascade agent access. Genuinely sufficient for solopreneurs who code occasionally rather than full-time.

**Trade-off vs Pro ($15/mo):** weaker agent on complex multi-file work, slower premium models.

### [Aider](/tools/aider) (free CLI + your own API key)

[Aider](/tools/aider) is open source. Free to use the tool itself; you pay for API tokens. Combine with [DeepSeek](/tools/deepseek) API ($0.14 per 1M input tokens) for genuinely cheap agent coding - $5-10/month covers most solopreneur usage.

### [GitHub Copilot](/tools/copilot-microsoft) Free

[GitHub Copilot](/tools/copilot-microsoft) ships a free tier in 2026: 2,000 completions/month + 50 chat messages/month. Enough for occasional coding.

## Image generation

### [Ideogram](/tools/ideogram) Free

[Ideogram](/tools/ideogram) free tier provides 25 prompts/week with full feature access. Genuinely the most generous free tier among production-quality image tools. For solopreneurs generating ~5-10 images per week (social posts, basic mockups), the free tier is sufficient.

### [Stable Diffusion](/tools/stable-diffusion) (open source, free with own GPU)

If you have a capable GPU (RTX 4060+), [Stable Diffusion](/tools/stable-diffusion) via Fooocus or ComfyUI is unlimited free generation. The setup takes 1-2 hours but the long-term cost is electricity only.

### [Canva AI](/tools/canva-ai) Free

[Canva AI](/tools/canva-ai) free tier includes Magic Studio image generation (50 lifetime credits, sometimes refreshed), basic Magic Resize, and the broader Canva design toolkit. The image-gen budget is small but the design tooling beyond AI is genuinely useful.

## Research

### [Perplexity](/tools/perplexity) Free

[Perplexity](/tools/perplexity) Free includes 5 Pro Searches/day and unlimited regular searches. The 5 Pro Searches are the high-value ones - for solopreneurs doing 1-2 deep research tasks per day, the free tier is sufficient.

**Trade-off vs Pro ($20/mo):** fewer Pro Searches, no file uploads, no Spaces.

### [Genspark](/tools/genspark) Free

[Genspark](/tools/genspark) Free tier provides ~30 daily credits with access to the Sparkpages format. Better than Perplexity Free for "give me a one-page research brief" outputs.

### [Google AI Studio](https://ai.google.dev) Free

For solopreneurs comfortable with API keys, Google AI Studio provides free access to Gemini 2.5 Pro for low-volume use (~50 requests/day). Pair with Aider or Cursor for free agent-mode coding.

## Notes and knowledge

### [Notion](https://notion.so) Free

The base [Notion](https://notion.so) free tier (without Notion AI add-on) covers personal workspaces, unlimited pages and blocks, basic databases, and 7-day version history. The free tier is genuinely sufficient for solo knowledge work.

For AI features, the Notion AI add-on costs $10/seat extra - skip it on the free tier and use Claude or Gemini free for the AI layer.

### [Obsidian](/tools/obsidian) (free for personal use)

[Obsidian](/tools/obsidian) is free for personal use, runs locally, and has 2,000+ community plugins including AI plugins (Smart Connections, Copilot, Text Generator) that connect to your own API keys.

### [Mem](/tools/mem-ai) Free

[Mem](/tools/mem-ai) ships a free trial period (4 weeks); not a sustainable free tier but useful for evaluating.

## Voice and meetings

### [Otter.ai](/tools/otter-ai) Free

[Otter.ai](/tools/otter-ai) free tier: 300 transcription minutes/month, 30 minutes/conversation. Sufficient for solopreneurs in 5-8 short meetings per week.

**Trade-off vs Pro ($10/mo):** lower minute caps, fewer AI summary features.

### [Fathom](/tools/fathom) Free

[Fathom](/tools/fathom) free tier is unlimited recording and transcription (one of the most generous in the category). The summaries are AI-generated and post to Slack/email.

### [ElevenLabs](/tools/elevenlabs) Free

[ElevenLabs](/tools/elevenlabs) free tier: 10,000 characters/month + voice cloning of your own voice. Sufficient for occasional podcast intros, voiceovers, or accessibility work.

## Automation

### [Make.com](/tools/make-com) Free

[Make.com](/tools/make-com) free tier: 1,000 operations/month + 2 active scenarios. Genuinely sufficient for connecting 3-4 tools (e.g., new Stripe payment → Notion + Email).

**Trade-off vs Core ($9/mo):** operation cap, scenario count, no premium apps.

### [Zapier](/tools/zapier) Free

[Zapier](/tools/zapier) free tier: 100 tasks/month + 5 single-step Zaps. Tighter than Make but the largest app integration library if your tools aren't on Make.

### [n8n](/tools/n8n) (self-hosted, free)

[n8n](/tools/n8n) is open source and free if you self-host (Docker on a $5/mo Hetzner VPS). For solopreneurs comfortable with self-hosting, it replaces both Make and Zapier with no per-operation cost.

## Translation and content

### [DeepL](/tools/deepl) Free

[DeepL](/tools/deepl) free tier: 500,000 characters/month, browser extension, and document translation. The best free translation quality for European languages.

### [Grammarly](/tools/grammarly) Free

[Grammarly](/tools/grammarly) free tier covers grammar, spelling, and basic clarity. Sufficient for most professional writing.

## Customer support

### [Tidio](/tools/tidio) Free

[Tidio](/tools/tidio) free tier: 50 AI chatbot conversations/month + unlimited live chat. Sufficient for early-stage solopreneur businesses.

### [HubSpot](/tools/hubspot) Free CRM

[HubSpot](/tools/hubspot) free CRM includes contact management, basic email marketing (2,000 emails/month), forms, and live chat. The "AI features" are sparse on the free tier but the CRM itself is genuinely capable.

For a complete $0/month stack:

- **Writing:** [Gemini](/tools/gemini) Free + [Claude](/tools/claude) Free
- **Research:** [Perplexity](/tools/perplexity) Free
- **Coding (if needed):** [Windsurf](/tools/windsurf) Free + [Copilot](/tools/copilot-microsoft) Free
- **Image:** [Ideogram](/tools/ideogram) Free + [Canva AI](/tools/canva-ai) Free
- **Notes:** [Notion](https://notion.so) Free + [Obsidian](/tools/obsidian) Free
- **Meetings:** [Fathom](/tools/fathom) Free
- **Automation:** [Make.com](/tools/make-com) Free
- **Email/CRM:** [HubSpot](/tools/hubspot) Free CRM
- **Grammar:** [Grammarly](/tools/grammarly) Free

Total: $0/month. Covers 70-80% of what a $200/mo paid stack does for a solopreneur with low to moderate volume.

## When to start paying

The first $20/month upgrade with the highest ROI: [Claude](/tools/claude) Pro at $20/mo. Removes the daily message cap, unlocks Projects, and is the single tool that compounds the most across writing, code, and research work.

The second $20/month upgrade: [Perplexity](/tools/perplexity) Pro at $20/mo. Citations + Pro Search become unlimited; for research-heavy roles this pays for itself in week one.

After $40/mo combined, the ROI on additional paid tools depends on which workflow you're scaling - design, video, or automation.

## What to avoid on free tiers

- **AI cold-email tools at free tiers**: deliverability is poor; you damage domain reputation faster than you book meetings.
- **AI website builders (Wix AI, etc.) at free tiers**: hosted on free subdomains, watermarked, slow page speed - the "free" cost is brand damage.
- **AI SEO tools at free tiers**: the free data is too limited to make decisions; better to commit to a paid month and run a real audit.
