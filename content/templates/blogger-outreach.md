# Blogger and Substack Outreach Template

Use this template to pitch tech bloggers, Substack writers, and newsletter operators who publish "best AI tools for X" round-ups. The goal is a contextual editorial backlink inside a future round-up where MytheAi is cited as the data source, plus optional co-marketing (cross-post, embed, guest data).

---

## Target selector heuristics

Send to bloggers and writers who match one or more of these signals:

1. Has published at least 2 "best AI tools for {{VERTICAL}}" round-ups in the last 12 months. They will publish more, and your data can shape the next one.
2. Has a Substack, Beehiiv, or Ghost newsletter with at least 1,000 subscribers (check subscriber badge or social proof) or a blog with visible monthly traffic over 5,000 (use SimilarWeb or Ahrefs check).
3. Writes for a specific vertical that overlaps with MytheAi categories: writing, coding, sales, marketing, design, healthcare, legal, HR, finance, customer support, research, productivity. Generic AI bloggers are lower priority than vertical specialists.
4. Has cited at least one external data source in a recent round-up (e.g. G2, ProductHunt, third-party benchmark). Linkers link.
5. Replies to reader comments or has a public email. Ghost authors with no engagement signal rarely respond to cold pitches.

Skip: pure AI-news aggregators, content-farm blogs with no author byline, sites with display ads on every paragraph (they are SEO-spam, your link will not carry weight).

---

## Subject line variants (A/B test 3 to 4 per outreach wave)

1. Data source for your next "best AI {{VERTICAL}}" round-up
2. 214 hand-tested AI tools - free data for your readers
3. Quick offer for the {{NEWSLETTER_NAME}} community
4. Co-marketing idea: comparison data + cross-promo

## Follow-up cadence

- Day 0: send initial pitch
- Day 5 (only if no reply): one short bump - "Wanted to bump this in case the data offer is useful for any upcoming piece. Happy to send the raw spreadsheet today if you say which vertical."
- Day 14 (only if still no reply): final note - "Last note. If a future round-up needs hand-tested data, my inbox is open."
- After day 14: stop. Move to re-pitch list. Re-pitch quarterly when MytheAi hits a new milestone (250 hand-tested, new money page category, etc.).

Re-check Ahrefs or Google Alerts weekly for "mytheai.com" + blog name to catch unannounced links.

## What to send if they reply yes

When a blogger says "send me the data for vertical X":

1. Send a Google Sheet or CSV with these columns per tool: slug, name, category, tagline, hand-tested (Y/N), test date, tester, pricing tier, free tier (Y/N), starting price, rating, review count, top use cases, top integrations, pros (3), cons (3), MytheAi URL (https://mytheai.com/tools/{slug}), official URL.
2. Include a one-paragraph methodology note ("data hand-collected by John Pham, founder of MytheAi. Every tool in this sheet was personally tested. Free to cite with attribution to mytheai.com.").
3. Set a calendar reminder for 30 days out to check if the post went live.
4. If they ask for an embed widget, point them to the relevant /compare or /best URL and offer to add UTM tracking so you can attribute traffic.

## Co-marketing tier (high-value bloggers)

For bloggers with 10k+ subscribers or strong vertical authority, offer one of these instead of a plain data citation:

- **Guest data deep-dive**: you write a piece on "{{vertical}} AI tooling state of the market 2026" using MytheAi data, MytheAi cross-posts an excerpt with full credit.
- **Newsletter sponsor swap**: free mention in a future MytheAi newsletter in exchange for a sponsor-style mention in theirs (always disclose, never paid).
- **Co-authored long-form**: 3,000 word piece, both bylines, published on both sites with canonical URL on the higher-DA domain (theirs). MytheAi gets a "Co-authored with John Pham of MytheAi" byline + footer link.
