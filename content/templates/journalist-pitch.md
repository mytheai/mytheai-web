# Journalist Pitch Template

Use this template to pitch tech journalists at TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired, Fast Company, Business Insider, Axios, Protocol, The Information, and similar tier-1 outlets. The goal is a dofollow editorial backlink from a high-authority domain, not a product placement.

---

## Target selector heuristics

Send to journalists whose recent work matches one or more of these signals:

1. Has published in the last 90 days on AI tooling, AI directories, AI adoption by SMBs, or "best AI tools" round-ups. Stale beats lead to low reply rates.
2. Has criticised pay-to-rank or SEO-spammy AI listicles in print or on social. These reporters are primed for an honest-data angle.
3. Covers founder stories or solo-operator businesses. The 214 hand-tested reviews is a story when framed as "one person, one year, no PR team".
4. Has a verifiable email address (not a generic tips line). Use Muck Rack, Twitter bio, or the publication staff page.
5. Has linked out to data sources before. Check 3 to 5 of their recent articles for outbound links to research, datasets, or product reviews. Linkers link.

Skip: pure crypto reporters, pure VC-funding reporters with no product angle, "best of CES" generalists, and any reporter whose last AI piece was over 6 months ago.

---

## Subject line variants (A/B test 3 to 4 per outreach wave)

1. I hand-tested 214 AI tools in one year - here is the data
2. Story idea: a directory that refuses pay-to-rank in a $200B AI market
3. Pitch: solo founder vs the pay-to-rank AI directory cartel
4. 593 AI tools, 214 hand-tested, zero affiliate-influenced rankings

Avoid: anything with "Hi!", emoji, or the word "amazing". Tier-1 reporters delete these on sight.

## Follow-up cadence

- Day 0: send initial pitch
- Day 4 (only if no reply, no auto-responder): one short bump - "Wanted to make sure this did not get buried. Happy to send the raw dataset if useful for any current piece."
- Day 10 (only if still no reply): final note - "Last note from me. If timing is off, no problem. If you ever want the raw data on AI directory transparency, my inbox is open."
- After day 10: stop. Three touches max. Add to a re-pitch list for the next major site milestone (e.g. 250 hand-tested, 1000 task pages).

Log every outreach in the affiliate-tracker.md sheet under a new "Press outreach" tab with these columns: date sent, journalist name, publication, subject line variant, reply received (Y/N), article published (Y/N), URL of article, dofollow vs nofollow, anchor text used. Check Ahrefs or Google Alerts weekly for "mytheai.com" + "John Pham" mentions to catch links the journalist forgot to email you about.
